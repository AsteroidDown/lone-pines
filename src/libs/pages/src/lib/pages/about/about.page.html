"Touching on inspiration ranging from the retro feelings of Electric Light Orchestra to the modern vibes of Bon Iver, lone pines stands out from the local Vancouver indie scene."

Lone pines members Callum Follett and Braden Williams first met in high school band class, knowing that there was musical potential between them. After performing under different monikers for the past 8 years, they settled on lone pines and released their first single You Are in late 2020.

The nostalgic vibes and chilling vocals garnered over 1000 views on YouTube and over 10,000 plays on Spotify. After success from their latest single Boys in LA, the band is excited to release their first full length album February 2023:

From the upbeat and catchy "Something New" to the more introspective "Say it out Loud," the album explores the emotions and sounds of a being lost in a strange new world. lone pines' ability to blend different genres into their music is on full display in tracks like "Runaway" and "Canâ€™t Get Enough of You," which seamlessly merge indie rock with elements of pop and electronic music.

The album speaks to the feelings of alienation we experience in a world dominated by capitalism, as well as the interpersonal relationships we navigate in our daily lives.
