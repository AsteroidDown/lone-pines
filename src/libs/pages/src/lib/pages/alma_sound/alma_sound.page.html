"Touching on inspiration ranging from the retro feelings of Electric Light Orchestra to the modern vibes of Bon Iver, lone pines stands out from the local Vancouver indie scene."

Lone pines members Callum Follett and Braden Williams first met in high school band class, knowing that there was musical potential between them. After performing under different monikers for the past 8 years, they settled on lone pines and released their first single You Are in late 2020.

The nostalgic vibes and chilling vocals garnered over 1000 views on YouTube and over 10,000 plays on Spotify. After success from their latest single Boys in LA, the band is excited to release their first full length album in early 2023.
